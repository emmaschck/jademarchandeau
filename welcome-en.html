Jade Marchandeau, a video artist and illustrator, was born in Paris in 1995.

She studied at the Institut Sup√©rieur des Arts de Toulouse (ISDAT) before pursuing her graduate studies at the Bezalel Academy of Art and Design (Tel Aviv, Israel-Palestine), where she graduated with a 91/100 average.

Jade's work focuses on her personal life, relationships and experiences. She often incorporates images of her family and friends, while questioning her identity through a fragmented editing technique and the use of voice-over. In this way, she emphasizes the symbolic or philosophical significance of her images. In addition, she pays particular attention to the elements of everyday life that are often considered insignificant. In this way, she claims the personal story as a catalyst of the common history and as a mirror of our present society.
